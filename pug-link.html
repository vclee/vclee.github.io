Every year, 2.7 million adoptable dogs and cats are euthanized in the United States.

There are simply too many pets, with shelters already at capacity, and too few people consider adoption when looking for a companion. There are so many reasons why you should adopt, including:

  • Because you'll save a life
  • Because you'll receive unconditional love
  • Because it'll cost you less
  • Because adoption helps more than just one animal
