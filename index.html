The Majestic Pug

This site is dedicated to this wrinkly, flat-faced, curly tailed breed.

Pugs Are Fascinating

Pugs are silly, charismatic little bundles of personality. They are short, stout, smooshed faced that snort and honk with their nose.

  • Pugs shed A LOT
  • A pack of pugs is called a Grumble
  • They are voracious eaters
  • Their flat faces put them at risk for eye injuries and breathing problems
  • Pugs are an ancient breed originating from China
  • They have donut shaped tails
  • Pugs snore

Pugs Are Always Hungry

Because Pugs have an unlimited appetite, they are easily susceptible to over eating and weight issues. Below is a feeding chart to help with how much to feed your little (or big) pug.

Weight Puppy Adult Senior
5-10lbs 0.75-1.25cup 0.50-1cup 0.50-0.75
10-20lbs 1.0.25-2cup 1.0-1.5cup 0.75-1.25cup
20-30lbs 2.0-3.0cup 1.5-2.0cup 1.25-1.75cup
